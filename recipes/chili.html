Chili Recipe

Description

A classic winter recipe that offers a near-blank template for experimentation Add or omit any beans you like; ground turkey, pork or beef; or even a chipotle chili or two.

Ingredients

  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 tbsp. tomato paste
  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 can black beans
  • cheddar cheese, for serving

Steps

  1. Cook onion until soft, 5 minutes, then add tomato paste stirring to combine.
  2. Add ground beef and cook, 7 minutes.
  3. Add broth, beans and spices. Pressure cook on high for 14 minutes.
  4. Slow release, then serve with shredded cheddar cheese.
