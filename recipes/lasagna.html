Lasagna Recipe

Description

Also known as "the World's Best Lasagna," this beef lasagna recipe is well worth the effort. Selecting only the best ingredients will ensure that your friends and family will go home still talking about this delicious recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • seasoning to taste

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef and garlic over medium heat until browned.
  2. Stir in tomato sauce and water. Season with sugar, parsley and salt.
  3. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  4. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg and salt.
  5. Assemble the lasagna and top with parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake in pre-heated oven at 375 F for 45 minutes.
