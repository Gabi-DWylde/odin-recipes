Potato Soup Recipe

Description

A simple but delicious recipe to warm you up in the cold winter days.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices bacon
  • 2 pounds russet potatoes
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 1/2 cups Half-n-Half
  • Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup Sour cream

Steps

  1. Cook bacon until crisp, 10-12 minutes. Set aside
  2. Cook onion, 5-7 minutes. Pour in broth.
  3. Add potatoes, Half-n-Half and salt and pepper to taste. Cover pot, then set to pressure cook on high for 8 minutes. Slow release pressure.
  4. Uncover pot and stir in sour cream. Use an immersion blender until only some chunky bits remain
  5. Top with chedar and bacon to serve.
